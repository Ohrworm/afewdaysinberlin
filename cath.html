A wander around Schillerkiez in Neukölln

Go for breakfast at the Schillerbarwhere they do a typical Berlin breakfast of cheesee, salami, fresh fruit and bröthen.

On a beautiful day take a walk down to the end of the street where you'll find Tempelhof park, an old airport. If it's a saturday, head over to the otherside of the park to join an English tour of the building and learn about it's fascinating, crazy history.

Walking back down to Hermannstrasse, pop into Treibholz for kaffee und kuchen.

On Flughafenstrasse you'll find a few second hand shops where you can pick up your preferred bit of stylish Berlin tat to take home.

For dinner, the cosiest little Italian restaurant is tucked away on Mainzerstrasse. It's called Sala da Mangiare and only has a few homemade pasta dishes on the menu each day. Make sure to book if it's a weekend, the restaurant is tiny.
